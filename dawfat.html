About DawFat DuaNest

DawFat DuaNest is designed to help Muslims worldwide connect with their faith through daily supplications. Whether you're looking for Duas for specific occasions or general daily prayers, our app ensures you have them at your fingertips.

In Loving Memory of Late Dawud Muhammad Wasaram

DawFat DuaNest is dedicated to the cherished memory of Late Dawud Muhammad Wasaram, the esteemed writer of the collection of Duas. His tireless efforts in compiling these supplications have left an everlasting impact on countless lives. May his legacy continue to inspire and guide us all. May Allah grant him Jannah, Ameen.
